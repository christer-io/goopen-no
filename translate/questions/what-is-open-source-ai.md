# What is open-source AI? 

**Answer:** Open source AI refers to artificial intelligence software, models, libraries, or frameworks that are made available to the public under open-source licenses. For an open AI solution to truly be open, all its code, models and training data must be under an open license. 

This means that anyone can view, use, modify, and distribute the source code, fostering collaboration and innovation within the AI community.

The term Open AI is now more a product name for the brand OpenAI.
