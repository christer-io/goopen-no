# How does licensing work for open hardware?

**Answer:** Open hardware projects often use licenses that specify the terms under which the hardware designs can be used and shared. Popular open hardware licenses include the Creative Commons licenses and the TAPR Open Hardware License. These licenses may require attribution, prohibit commercial use, or allow for derivative works, depending on the specific license chosen.
