# What are the advantages of using open source AI tools over proprietary ones? 

Open source AI tools provide transparency, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness. Users can inspect the code, customize it to their needs, and avoid vendor lock-in. Plus, the community support often results in rapid updates and bug fixes.
