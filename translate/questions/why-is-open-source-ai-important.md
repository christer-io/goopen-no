# Why is open source AI important? 

Open source AI promotes transparency, collaboration, and accessibility in the development of AI technologies. It allows developers worldwide to work together, share knowledge, and build upon each other's work, accelerating the advancement of AI and making it more inclusive.

Transparency is important in all technology development, but for AI it is crucial,  because of the complex nature of many projects. Releasing all components under an open license, including the training data, ensures accountability and transparency.
