# Are there any licensing or legal considerations when using open source AI?

**Answer:** Yes, it's crucial to understand the licensing terms of open source AI projects you use. Each project may have a different license, and you should ensure compliance with its requirements to avoid legal issues.

For a project to use the word "open" or "free" in the product title or communication around the product does not give any guarantee that the project is truly open.
