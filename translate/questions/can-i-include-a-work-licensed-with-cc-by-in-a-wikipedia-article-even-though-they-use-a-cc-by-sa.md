#### Can I include a work licensed with CC BY in a Wikipedia article even though they use a CC BY-SA license?

**Answer:** Yes. Works licensed under CC BY may be incorporated into works that are licensed under CC BY-SA. For example, you may incorporate a CC BY photograph into a Wikipedia article so long as you keep all copyright notices intact, provide proper attribution, and otherwise comply with the terms of CC BY. Learn more about the licenses.
