# Can I use open source AI for commercial applications? 

**Answer:** Yes, you can use open-source AI for commercial purposes. Many open-source AI licenses, such as the Apache License and MIT License, permit commercial use. However, you may need to comply with certain license requirements, such as proper attribution.
