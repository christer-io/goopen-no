## Learning goals

- Translate open principles into contribution workflows.
- Define lightweight governance expectations.
- Improve contributor onboarding and decision clarity.

## Core ideas

Open collaboration works best when teams combine transparent decision-making with clear participation paths.

Practical building blocks:

- Public roadmap and issue tracking
- Clear contribution guidelines and review process
- Documented decision rules
- Frequent releases and feedback loops

These support inclusive meritocracy by making it easier for new contributors to participate and succeed.

## Why it matters

Strong governance reduces bottlenecks and conflict while keeping projects welcoming and resilient.

## Practice

Draft a one-page contributor workflow for your project: from first issue to merged contribution.
