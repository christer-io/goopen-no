## Learning goals

- Use the OSI license list effectively.
- Understand license categories and SPDX identifiers.
- Compare common license families for real-world use.

## Core ideas

The OSI licenses page is more than a list. It includes:

- License names
- SPDX IDs (important for tooling and compliance automation)
- Categories such as Popular / Strong Community, Superseded, and Special Purpose

A practical approach is to start with commonly adopted licenses and avoid unnecessary novelty unless your use case requires it.

## Why it matters

Choosing a well-understood license lowers risk for adopters, contributors, procurement teams, and legal review.

## Practice

Create a shortlist of 2-3 candidate licenses for a new project and note why each is a fit.
