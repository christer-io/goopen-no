## Learning goals

- Plan a phased open source adoption path.
- Reduce legal and process ambiguity early.
- Define measurable success criteria.

## Core ideas

A practical 30-day plan:

1. **Week 1**: confirm project scope, rights ownership, and target license candidates.
2. **Week 2**: publish contribution guide, code of conduct, and issue templates.
3. **Week 3**: make first public release and invite feedback.
4. **Week 4**: review participation metrics and refine governance.

Track outcomes such as response time, contribution quality, and release cadence.

## Why it matters

Open source success is not just legal compliance. It is a repeatable operational model that balances openness, quality, and sustainability.

## Practice

Write your own 30-day checklist with owners, dates, and one success metric per week.
