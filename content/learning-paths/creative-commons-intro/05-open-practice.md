## Learning goals

- Identify where CC supports OER and open culture initiatives.
- Describe benefits and risks institutions should plan for.
- Define a realistic first implementation step.

## Core ideas

In education, CC enables adaptation and sharing of open educational resources. In cultural heritage (GLAM and related institutions), CC and public domain tools support access, reuse, and visibility of collections.

Successful open practice usually combines:

- Policy alignment
- Clear rights metadata
- Staff workflows
- Community collaboration

## Why it matters

Open practice is not only a licensing decision. It is an operational model that improves access, participation, and long-term relevance.

## Practice

Write a 30-day action plan with one concrete pilot:

- One collection, lesson, or resource set to open
- One license/public domain decision
- One publication channel
- One success metric
