## Learning goals

- Apply a CC license to your own material.
- Reuse CC-licensed works with correct attribution.
- Avoid common remix and compatibility mistakes.

## Core ideas

A practical creator workflow:

1. Confirm you control the rights for the material.
2. Choose the license that matches your sharing goals.
3. Add clear license and attribution metadata where the work is published.

A practical reuse workflow:

1. Verify license terms and version.
2. Check whether your intended reuse is allowed.
3. Provide attribution (title/creator/source/license where possible).
4. Mark changes when you adapt.

## Why it matters

Open licensing is most effective when permissions and obligations are both clear. Good attribution and clear labeling improve trust and legal certainty.

## Practice

Create a sample attribution line for an image you want to reuse in a presentation.
