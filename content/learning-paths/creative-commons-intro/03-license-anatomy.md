## Learning goals

- Understand the main CC license conditions.
- Compare license openness across the CC suite.
- Recognize enforceability and compatibility considerations.

## Core ideas

CC licenses combine a small set of conditions:

- **BY** (Attribution): credit is required.
- **SA** (ShareAlike): adaptations must keep the same license.
- **NC** (NonCommercial): no commercial use.
- **ND** (NoDerivatives): sharing allowed, modification restricted.

Public domain and dedication tools (for example CC0) sit outside the main license family and are useful when maximal reuse is intended.

## Why it matters

Different choices produce different downstream behavior. For example, ND limits remix, while SA encourages open reciprocity in derivatives.

## Practice

Pick two licenses (for example CC BY and CC BY-NC-SA) and list one benefit and one tradeoff for each.
